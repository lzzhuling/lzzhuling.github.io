About Me

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Professor Anthony Man-Cho So at CUHK. I received a B.S. in mathematics in 2017 from Zhejiang University and M.S. in computational mathematics in 2020 from Zhejiang University under the supervision of Professor Chong Li. I was a research assistant under the mentorship of Professor Xiaoqi Yang during February 2019 to May 2019 at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
